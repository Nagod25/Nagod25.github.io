What is StashBox?

STASHBOX is a savings and investment platform where you save or invest; save with or without tenure; in Naira or USD; short term or long term; with card or wallet; save individually or in groups. StashBox is a product of Stash Fintech Ltd incorporated in Nigeria with RC 1467750. Stash Fintech Ltd is in partnership with Afrinvest Trustees Ltd, a duly licensed company by the Security and Exchange Commission (SEC) in Nigeria. Stash Fintech Ltd is related to Stash Fintech Cooperative Ltd, a microfinance institution registered with FCT, Abuja. STASHBOX is a combination of two words “Stash” and “Box”. Stash means to store something somewhere. A Box is a place to keep valuable items secretly and securely. Therefore, STASHBOX is a digital box where you store or stash funds securely. STASHBOX is like an Omnibus. An omnibus is a bus with many rooms in it. StashBox, like an omnibus allows you to save, invest and enjoy other financial services all in one app. All processes are designed to be contactless, paperless and 100% end to end. On-lending refers to loans to pharmaceuticals, employees of the federal government; individuals who want to acquire assets like cars.

    Add Card

    Click the side menu. From side menu, click Payments. From Payments, click Add Card, a payment page appears where your card can be linked. The following field are to be populated with the card details:

    There are two payment types:

  • PAN: A payment card number, primary account number (PAN) or simply a card number, is the card identifier found on payment cards such as credit cards or debit cards. It is a 16–19-digit number card details that appears on the debit card e.g., 1234 5678 9012 3456 It is usually a 16-digit number for Visa and Master Card cards
  • Expiry Date: Date and month of card expiry e.g., 02/23
  • CVV: Card Verification Value Code e.g., 345: A three-digit number found at the back of the card. It is unique to each card.
  • PIN: Personal Identification Number e.g., 1234. A four-digit PIN which may be required during card verification process
  • SMS OTP Code: This is a One Time Password sent to the email or SMS of the cardholder during verification. It can be a 6-digit code e.g., 123446 depending on the card type Once the SMS OTP Code is inputted on the StashBox app for verification, the card is activated.

    • Card activation comes with a N50 debit passed to the account holder. The N50 is an automatic charge by the payment processor to validate and store the card for subsequent authorized usage. StashBox does not store cardholder details. Upon successful N50 debit, the card is activated on the app and stored for daily, weekly or monthly plan activation. Visa, Master, Verve, AMEX and all types of cards can be added. Multiple cards can be added on the same account. Added Cards can be activated or deactivated on the app. Any of the activated cards can be used to fund the savings plan. The payment page is powered by Paystack Inc, a licensed financial technology company by Central Bank of Nigeria to process payments. Card details, card related data are stored with Paystack and not on the StashBox app.

    Risk and Mitigants

    The name of the cardholder MUST match the verified withdrawal bank account to avoid fraud. No third-party transfer is allowed on StashBox as it is an in-bound app only. We encourage all StashBox users to use real names and not nicknames or short names to avoid restrictions on the account. Users will be charged 100 Naira for linking their card to the app. This confirms a successful linkage. This process applies to all the plans except for the Casual and Emerald Plan. The casual plan is a one-off savings.

    Create Virtual Account/Wallet: This links to creating virtual account. To create a virtual account, enter your BVN and submit the form. The system validates your BVN and if correct, it uses your existing BVN information to open a virtual account for you. Upon creating the virtual account, the virtual account is displayed on the Homepage. The virtual account is the wallet account. The virtual account is a 10-digit NUBAN account number from Wema, Sterling, Providus, Titan Trust or any commercial bank in Nigeria. Transfers can be made from any bank into this virtual bank account for the purchase of funding the savings, investment or loan repayment plans only. Asides StashBox , this account cannot be used for any other purpose. The account is in-bound only. Transfers, cash deposit, standing payment orders etc. can be made into the account, therefore making it in-bound. Withdrawals cannot be made from this account over the counter. You can only create one virtual account with STASHBOX for all your transaction. The wallet itself is not a plan. It is a transit account. If you don’t debit the wallet to create a plan, the money is still your money but it doesn’t accrue any interest even if it spends one year in your wallet. In case you can’t provide your BVN, you can skip that option but you will need to input your information manually. Once you do that, a virtual account will be created for you.

    Referral Bonus

    When you refer someone, we will pay you N1,000 Naira for every referral and active saver. If you refer someone and the person uses the code but has not started saving, you don’t get any bonus.     Top Up Savings

    Here, you add to your existing savings plan. Unlike creating a new savings plan with new tenor, new principal; when you top up, you add an amount to the existing savings principal but the initial tenor and recurring plans are still the same. It is ideal when you miss out on some repayment and you want to add up. This also happens when you have some spare funds and you do not want to be tempted to spend it. Therefore, you add to your existing savings principal but the tenor and the existing recurring plans remain the same.

    Transactions

    All transactions are captured as Deposit, Withdrawal to Wallet and Withdrawal to Bank under the Transaction section. Deposits are coloured in green font and arrow. It means funds entering via daily, weekly, monthly or casual plans. Funds moving from card or bank account INTO the app or plans. Withdrawal To Wallet are coloured in blue fonts and arrow. All withdrawal from savings/investment plans are first moved into the app wallet. From the wallet, user can decide whether to withdraw to their bank account, reinvest or restart a new savings plan. Withdrawn to Account are coloured in red fonts and arrows. This is withdrawal from wallet to your bank account already linked to the app. Your bank account already linked to under the Account Section is the recipient account here.

    Withdrawal

    Users withdraw from their savings plans into their wallet from this section. From the wallet (under Payments), users can withdraw into their bank account. Users can withdraw from savings or investment plans here.
